Addon code made by Halogen with art by Yhorm, Halogen, and quackk for Cubyz version 0.0.0

ores:
 - sapphire: generates above z=50, decreases swing time by 33% when not touching metal
 - emerald: generates z=-800..50, increases durability by 30% when next to another emerald, for a total of 50%
 - opal: generates in the root
 - lonsdaleite: generates in craters, higher toughness than uranium

biome:
 - crater: small biome that contains magma, lonsdaleite, and lots of iron

blocks:
 - sapphire block
 - cut sapphire block
 - emerald block
 - cut emerald block
 - opal block
 - cut opal block
 - lonsdaleite block
 - cut lonsdaleite block

recipes:
 - 4 sapphire <-> 1 sapphire block
 - 1 sapphire block --> 1 cut sapphire block
 - 1 cut sapphire block --> 4 sapphire
 - 4 emerald <-> 1 emerald block
 - 1 emerald block --> 1 cut emerald block
 - 1 cut emerald block --> 4 emerald
 - 4 opal <-> 1 opal block
 - 1 opal block --> 1 cut opal block
 - 1 cut opal block --> 4 opal
 - 4 lonsdaleite <-> 1 lonsdaleite block
 - 1 lonsdaleite block --> 1 cut lonsdaleite block
 - 1 cut lonsdaleite block --> 4 lonsdaleite
